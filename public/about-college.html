Einstein Academy of Technology and Management

Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India

Overview

Established in 2006, the Einstein Academy of Technology and Management (EATM) is a premier engineering institution located in Baniatangi, Bhubaneswar. The college is affiliated with the Biju Patnaik University of Technology (BPUT) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi.

Academic Excellence

EATM is dedicated to shaping the future of engineering professionals by providing high-quality technical education. After a common foundation year, students specialize in core branches including Computer Science, Data Science, Electrical & Electronics, Civil, and Mechanical Engineering. The campus is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, a vast library, and modern infrastructure to support innovation and research.
